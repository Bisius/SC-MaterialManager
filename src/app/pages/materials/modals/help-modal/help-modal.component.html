// How to use
01 Select Preferred Stations
  • › Click the STATIONS button in the action bar.
  • › Toggle stations on or off — active stations are highlighted and used to filter locations throughout the app.
  • › With no stations selected, all locations are available everywhere.
02 Add Custom Locations
  • › Click the LOCATIONS button in the action bar.
  • › Enter a name and system for your custom location, then confirm. Custom locations appear alongside built-in ones and are included in the active-station dropdown.
03 Add Materials
  • › Click ADD MATERIAL in the action bar.
  • › Select the material type, enter quality (0–1000) and quantity (cSCU).
  • › For the location, type to search or focus the field to browse your active stations. Active stations appear highlighted at the top of the results.
  • › Toggle Show manifest after adding to jump straight to the cargo view after each entry.
04 Remove / Use / Move Materials
  • › Delete — click the × button on a record or group to remove it. An UNDO DELETE button appears in the action bar to restore the last removed item(s).
  • › Use — click the flask icon on a record to log consumption. Enter the amount used; if the remaining quantity reaches zero the record is automatically removed.
  • › Move — check one or more records, choose a destination in the transfer toolbar that appears, then click TRANSFER.
05 Import / Export Data
  • › Export — click the download icon in the toolbar to save all records as a .json file.
  • › Import — click the upload icon, select a previously exported file and choose Merge (adds to existing records) or Replace (overwrites everything).
}
